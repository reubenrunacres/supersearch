How to Read This

  • Each dot represents one known superconducting material
  • X & Y axes represent composition space (PCA of elemental properties like atomic mass, radius, valence)
  • Color gradient shows critical temperature: Blue (low Tc) → Purple → Pink (high Tc)
  • Red-outlined regions are statistically under-explored areas adjacent to known superconductors
  • Hover over dots to see material properties
  • Clusters represent composition families with similar elemental characteristics
