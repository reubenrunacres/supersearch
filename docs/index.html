SuperSearch

Built for Periodic Labs Autonomous Discovery

This 3D visualization maps 21,263 superconducting materials to reveal unexplored composition spaces with high discovery potential — directly supporting Periodic Labs' mission to accelerate materials discovery through autonomous experimentation.

Strategic Value for Periodic Labs

Complements GNoME Predictions
Identifies experimental gaps in composition space that GNoME predictions haven't yet covered, guiding where to synthesize next.
Autonomous Lab Prioritization
10 high-opportunity regions (red/yellow boxes) ranked by proximity to high-Tc materials and exploration density — ready for synthesis queue.
Hypothesis Generation
Visualizes composition families (convex hulls) to reveal promising interpolation targets between known high-Tc materials.

Methodology

Data Source: 21,263 superconductors from UCI SuperConductor dataset

Dimensionality Reduction: PCA projection from 81 atomic features to 2D composition space (X/Y axes)

Clustering: K-means (k=3) to identify composition families

Critical Temperature: Z-axis represents Tc (0-185K), color gradient from blue (low) to pink (high)

Sparse Region Detection: Grid-based density analysis to find under-explored composition spaces

Opportunity Ranking: Composite score based on proximity to high-Tc materials (Tc > 100K) and exploration density
