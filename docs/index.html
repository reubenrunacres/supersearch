SuperSearch

SuperSearch maps 21,263 known superconducting materials in composition space to identify under-explored regions for discovery.

Using PCA dimensionality reduction and DBSCAN clustering, we identify 3 major composition families and 34 sparse regions adjacent to high-Tc materials—potential targets for autonomous laboratory screening.

Materials analyzed: 21,263
Composition families: 3
Under-explored regions: 34
Tc range: 0-185K
Variance retained (2D): 73.55%

The 34 highlighted regions show composition spaces with fewer known materials, adjacent to superconductors with high critical temperatures. These represent opportunities for systematic experimental screening.

Built for Periodic Labs to demonstrate superconductor composition space analysis and autonomous lab targeting strategy.
